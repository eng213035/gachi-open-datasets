REQUIRED ATTRIBUTION WHEN YOU REDISTRIBUTE OR BUILD ON THIS DATA
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. State that you are using data made available by the Public Transportation
   Open Data Center ("本データセットは公共交通オープンデータセンターにおいて
   提供されるデータを利用して作成しています").
2. State that accuracy and completeness of the data are not guaranteed by
   ODPT, its data-providing operators, or gachi-tokusuru.
3. State the snapshot/extraction date (see metadata.json `last_updated` /
   the README's "本データセットは○年○月時点のスナップショット" line).
4. Do not direct inquiries about the data to the transit operators
   themselves — direct them to gachi-tokusuru (contact via
   https://api.gachi-tokusuru.com), per ODPT's developer guidelines.
5. Also cite gachi-tokusuru as the entity-resolution/dataset publisher
   (see each dataset's README "Cite as" section).

Individual source data (e.g. Tokyo Metropolitan Bureau of Transportation
records) may separately be available under plain CC BY 4.0 as noted by
their operator-level catalog page on ODPT. Screenshots of the relevant
catalog/license pages as of the harvest date are kept in
docs/odpt-license-screenshots/ for provenance.

conflicting statement elsewhere in this repo for the dataset CSVs
