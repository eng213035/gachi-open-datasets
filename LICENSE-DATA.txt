REQUIRED ATTRIBUTION WHEN YOU REDISTRIBUTE OR BUILD ON THIS DATA
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. State that you are using data made available by the Public Transportation
   Open Data Center ("本データセットは公共交通オープンデータセンターにおいて
   提供されるデータを利用して作成しています").
2. State that accuracy and completeness of the data are not guaranteed by
   ODPT, its data-providing operators, or gachi-tokusuru.
3. State the snapshot/extraction date (see metadata.json `last_updated` /
   the README's "本データセットは○年○月時点のスナップショット" line).
4. Do not direct inquiries about the data to the transit operators
   themselves — direct them to gachi-tokusuru (contact via
   https://api.gachi-tokusuru.com), per ODPT's developer guidelines.
5. Also cite gachi-tokusuru as the entity-resolution/dataset publisher
   (see each dataset's README "Cite as" section).

Individual source data (e.g. Tokyo Metropolitan Bureau of Transportation
records) may separately be available under plain CC BY 4.0 as noted by
their operator-level catalog page on ODPT. Screenshots of the relevant
catalog/license pages as of the harvest date are kept in
docs/odpt-license-screenshots/ for provenance.

ADDITIONAL SOURCES FOR station-master v2 (nationwide stations)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

The nationwide stations added in station-master v2 (station_id st_00426 and up)
are derived from the following sources, in addition to ODPT:

1. MLIT 国土数値情報（鉄道データ） N02-24 — nationwide station geometry, line
   names and operators. Published free for general use under the 国土数値情報
   利用約款 (CC BY 4.0-compatible; https://nlftp.mlit.go.jp/ksj/other/agreement.html).
   Required attribution, reproduced when you redistribute:

       「国土数値情報（鉄道データ）」（国土交通省）を加工して作成
       (Processed from MLIT National Land Numerical Information — Railway data,
        N02-24. Source: https://nlftp.mlit.go.jp/ksj/ )

   The result is processed/derived data and must not be presented as if it were
   an official government product. See docs/n02-license-check.md.

2. Wikidata (https://www.wikidata.org/) — English station names (the `name`
   column where `name_source` is `wikidata`). Released under CC0 1.0 (public
   domain dedication); no attribution is legally required, but we credit
   Wikidata and its contributors.

3. kuromoji (IPADIC) — machine Hepburn transliteration used only for the `name`
   column where `name_source` is `romanized`. This is our own processing, not a
   third-party dataset.
