SOURCES AND REQUIRED ATTRIBUTION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Vacancy counts — Housing and Land Survey (住宅・土地統計調査), Statistics
   Bureau of Japan (総務省統計局), obtained via e-Stat (政府統計の総合窓口).
   e-Stat content is provided under the Government of Japan Standard Terms of
   Use (政府標準利用規約 第2.0版), which are compatible with CC BY 4.0.
   Required source credit (as redistributed):

       出典：政府統計の総合窓口(e-Stat)「住宅・土地統計調査」（総務省統計局）
       Source: Housing and Land Survey, Statistics Bureau of Japan, via e-Stat
       (survey: https://www.stat.go.jp/data/jyutaku/ ; portal: https://www.e-stat.go.jp/).

   The `total_dwellings`, `vacant_total` and `vacant_other` columns are official
   published values reproduced verbatim. `vacancy_rate` is computed by
   gachi-tokusuru from those published counts (see README) and is not an
   official e-Stat value.

2. Municipality codes, names, kana readings, and merger (改廃) records —
   総務省「全国地方公共団体コード」および「都道府県コード及び市区町村コード改正
   一覧表」(Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications). Provided under the
   Government of Japan Standard Terms of Use (CC BY 4.0 compatible). English
   names for municipalities dissolved before 2023 are Hepburn romanisations of
   the official Soumu kana readings.

3. Municipality coordinates — 国土地理院 (Geospatial Information Authority of
   Japan, GSI) Address Search API. Representative points, not polygon centroids.

4. Station bridge — English station names and station_id via the Japan Station
   Master in this repository, itself derived from the Public Transportation Open
   Data Center (ODPT); see ../LICENSE-DATA.txt.
